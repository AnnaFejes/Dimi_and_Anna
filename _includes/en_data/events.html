Events

  • Ceremony

    17:00

    The wedding ceremony - watch out at the end if any of them is stepping on the other's foot - Bulgarians believe whoever steps on the others foot is the one calling the shots! After the ceremony a glass will be broken - the number of broken pieces symbolize the longevity of the marriage

  • Reception

    18:45

    Starting of the reception & gala dinner. During the evening, the traditional Bulgarian bread will be broken between the newlywed couple - Bulgarians believe, the person getting the bigger piece is the one in charge.

  • First dance

    19:10

    The first dance of the newlyweds

  • Stealing of the bride

    20:20

    This is a tradition from both Hungary & Bulgaria - usually the husband & groomsmen have to do various tasks or bribe the brides family & friends to return her. Bulgarians do tend to this prior the ceremony, however Hungarians do this as part of the evening festivities.

  • Cake

    21:20

    Cutting the cake - pay attention to whose hands are on top of the knife cutting the cake, as Hungarians believe the person who makes the decisions has their hand on top of the other.

  • Dancing & Music

    22:25

    During the evening, Bulgarian folklore dancers & the Bulgarians will show you how to dance "hora" - now is the time to practice it!
