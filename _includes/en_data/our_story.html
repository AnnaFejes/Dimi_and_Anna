Our Story

2018 Anna let it slip that she had feelings for someone - Dimi even suggested to kidnap one of our friends to tell who it was... Surprise: it was him

2019 Our first holiday to Utrecht - we went to see Dermot Kennedy and really enjoyed our time in the Netherlands.

2020 We moved in together officially into the room in 35 Waverley Road, just before the first Covid lockdown.

2021 After being apart due Anna being stuck in Ireland for a while for work, we moved into our first home together in Saffron Walden - it was tiny, but it was ours and we also got our little red Yaris that took us on many adventures.

2022 We started camping, and had went on multiple trips both within and outside the country. Changed jobs that allowed us more freedom to travel.

2023 We have visited Stockholm for the first time trying to get Dimi into skiing, which resulted in a 500+ days of Duolingo streak for Swedish and we also climbed the Peak to Peak trail in Madeira with incredible views.

2024 We have got our first house in Royston, flew to Japan which was our dream destination even before we were together and Dimi proposed on the darkest night of the year to make it forever the brightest for us - Anna said YES.
