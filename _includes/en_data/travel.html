Travel

Plane

The nearest airport is SOF in Sofia. British Airways, Bulgaria Air (both from London Heathrow), WizzAir (from London Luton) and Ryanair (from London Stansted, Birmingham & Edinburgh) flies there directly from the UK. Other, alternative airports are BOJ in Bourgas and VAR in Varna, both close to the sea. WizzAir serves both from London Gatwick & Luton, and Jet2.com serves Bourgas from various airports

Public Transport

There are bus services operated by Ardatur and Flixbus between Budapest and Sofia (and Plovdiv), some of the coaches originate in Germany. There is also an option to travel to the Bulgarian capital by train from Hungary, but with multiple changes via Romania in the following route: Budapest-Keleti station - Videle (Romania) - Ruse (Bulgaria) - Sofia.
